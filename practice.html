Controls

WASD · Shift · Click

Move with WASD. Hold Shift to sprint.

Left-click on whatever your crosshair is on to pick it up or use it.

Stamina runs out. Don't sprint from everything.

The Exit

Exit door
Key
Crowbar

The door has three blockages: a padlock, wooden boards, and a fingerprint keypad.

Find the key to break the padlock. Find the crowbar to rip off the boards.

Each use opens a quiz: 10 easy questions, 0 wrong — or 5 hard, 1 wrong allowed. Fail and you lose the tool. It respawns somewhere else in the dark.

The Mannequins

Mannequin
Red one
Gun

Mannequins freeze while you look at them. Look away and they advance — faster than you walk, slower than you sprint.

The red one drops a keycard for the keypad when shot enough times with the gun.

Using the keycard: 8 hard questions, 0 wrong. Any wrong answer and the red mannequin respawns. You'll have to kill it again.

Death

If a mannequin catches you, the screen goes black. You have 50 seconds to answer 3 easy questions.

All three right — you wake up. All progress saved. Ammo, inventory, kills — everything.

One wrong answer or the clock runs out: the shift restarts. From the beginning.

You win by killing the red mannequin and clearing all three blockages on the exit door.
